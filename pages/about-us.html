Our Story

Empowering HR Professionals
Worldwide

We are on a mission to transform how human resources professionals connect, learn, and grow by building the most robust, supportive, and innovative community in the world.

2018

The Year We Started

What began as an invite-only forum of 50 visionary leaders has grown into India's most powerful networking ecosystem for human resources professionals.

How It All Began

The HR landscape was incredibly fragmented. Professionals were working in silos, struggling to find reliable mentors, and spending countless hours reinventing the wheel on policies, compliance, and hiring strategies.

IHR was born out of a simple idea: What if we brought everyone together? We started as a small, invite-only forum of 50 HR leaders sharing best practices. Today, we are India's largest HR network, connecting thousands of professionals with premium resources, lateral hiring support, and world-class events.

  • Started by HR Professionals, for HR Professionals
  • Fostering a Culture of Continuous Learning
  • Driving Innovation in Talent Acquisition
